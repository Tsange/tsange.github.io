Resources

Maps

  • GeoJSON Regions - Downloadable continental maps at different resolutions
  • High Charts - Collection of maps from individual countries or regions

UK Specific

  • UK-GeoJSON - TopoJSON Maps for Parliamentary Constituencies, Local Wards, National Assemblies and European Electoral Regions
  • Ordnance Survey - Generates Maps for the web from GeoJSON, TopoJSON or ESRI Shapefiles
  • DATA.GOV.UK - All UK boundary divisions
  • SketchMap - Generate UK maps using a series of overlays that can be shared or downloaded as a PDF
  • OldUKMaps - My collection of historic maps   
    • /Maps/OldUKMaps

Tools

  • GeoJSONLint - GeoJSON validator
  • GeoJSON-TopoJSON - Convert GeoJSON to TopoJSON and vice versa
  • The Distillery - Change a GeoJSON map's projection and simplify geometry
  • MapShaper - Simplify and repair line intersections
  • Map Starter - Generates Maps for the web from GeoJSON, TopoJSON or ESRI Shapefiles

Information Sources

  • World DataBank - General
  • Wikipedia - List of national statistic gathering organisations by country
  • World Health Organization

UK Specific

  • DATA.GOV.UK - Datasets on a wide variety of topics (Transport, Health, Economy etc.)
  • HSCIC - Data relating to health and social care 
  • ONS (Office of National Statistics) - Huge collection of data on the UK's economy, society and population.
  • UK Data Archive - Collection of research data in the social sciences and humanities
  • UK Data Service - Social, economic and population
  • WhatDoTheyKnow - Data obtained via Freedom of Information requests
